About me

I am a graduate student at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor pursuing a Master of Health Informatics (MHI) degree within the School of Public Health and the School of Information. I expect to graduate in May 2016. I was born and raised in Michigan, with most of my family members living near the Metro Detroit area. While I'm a Michigander at heart, I've also spent a good deal of my free time exploring the US and the world.

Education

I have attended the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor for all of my post-secondary education. In 2014 I received an undergraduate BA degree with concentrations in both Biopsychology, Cognition, and Neuroscience (BCN) and Political Science. I also received a minor in German Language. Throughout my undergraduate degree, I studied several disparate and multifaceted disciplines including the biological basis of human behavior, legislative policy development, environmental politics, and german business principles. I am currently pursuing a graduate degree in Health Informatics (MHI) at Michigan, with specific focus in program management, data analytics, and patient-facing decision support systems.
